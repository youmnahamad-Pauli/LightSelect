## General Notes

All luminaire lumen outputs in this schedule are **delivered lumen outputs** (after any diffuser, lens, or louvre losses) measured at operating temperature. Maintain the specified CCT throughout each zone; different CCT zones must not be intervisible. All products to be new, fit-for-purpose and supplied with all accessories required for a complete installation.
